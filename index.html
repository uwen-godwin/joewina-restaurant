Our Story

Welcome to Jowina Restaurant

Founded in 2014, Jowin Restaurant has been serving exceptional cuisine that combines traditional flavors with modern culinary techniques. Our mission is to create an unforgettable dining experience through outstanding food, impeccable service, and a warm atmosphere.

Chef Violet Ashua and his team take pride in sourcing the finest local ingredients to create dishes that delight all the senses. From farm to table, every ingredient is selected with care to ensure freshness and quality.
