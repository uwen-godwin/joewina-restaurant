About Us

Our Story

Joewina Restaurant was founded in 2010 by Chef Joseph Wina, with a vision to create an unforgettable dining experience that combines culinary excellence with warm hospitality.

Over the years, we've established ourselves as one of the premier dining destinations in the area, known for our innovative menu that blends traditional recipes with modern techniques.

Our Philosophy

At Joewina, we believe that great food starts with great ingredients. We source locally whenever possible and build relationships with farmers and suppliers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen is prepared with care and attention to detail, ensuring that your dining experience is nothing short of exceptional.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Joseph Wina

With over 20 years of experience in some of the world's finest restaurants, Chef Joseph brings passion and creativity to every dish. His culinary journey has taken him across Europe and Asia, influences you'll find throughout our menu.
